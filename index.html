PhD student in quantum computing @ Freie Universität Berlin

Email Google Scholar

I am a PhD student in Jens Eisert's group at the Dahlem Center for Complex Quantum Systems, Freie Universität Berlin.

My research sits at the intersection of quantum information theory, learning theory, and complexity theory. I explore questions like: What resources are required to learn about a specific aspect of an unknown quantum system? I am also interested in classical simulation of quantum computation, verification of quantum computation, quantum algorithms and quantum error correction.

You can get in touch with me via email.
